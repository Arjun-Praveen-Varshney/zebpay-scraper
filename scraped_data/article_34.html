Search New support ticket Check ticket status Solution home Zebpay Support Articles Crypto Deposit & Withdrawals Cancel or Reverse transactions Print Modified on: Thu, 19 Dec, 2019 at 4:38 PM Bitcoin and other cryptocurrency transactions can’t be cancelled or reversed as they are directly connected to the network.Bitcoin and cryptocurrency network transactions are designed to be irreversible and we don’t have control over them. Please ensure that you verify all transaction details prior to sending your sending your crypto to any receiving address.If you have sent cryptocurrency to an incorrect address by mistake, you may not be able to recover these coins/tokens. If you know and have the contact information of the person who controls that address, you can contact the receiver and ask for the cryptocurrency to be returned.
