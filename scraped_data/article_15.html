ZebPay will support the BitTorrent (BTT) token migration 1:1000 redenomination plan and renaming plan.


Complete details about the same are as below:


  • Deposits and withdrawals of BTT will be suspended on 16 January 2022 at 12:00 PM (IST).

  • All BTT trading pairs (BTT/INR, BTT/USDT) will be delisted on 17 January 2022 at 12:00 PM (IST) and all pending orders will be cancelled.

  • ZebPay will recover all BTT balances and begin the distribution of the new BitTorrent token. 

  • The new BTT token will be named BTTC and will be distributed to the users in the ratio of 1 BTT (Old) = 1000 BTTC. 

  • The old BitTorrent tokens will be renamed to BitTorrent (OLD).

  • Trading will open for BTTC/INR and BTTC/USDT on 25 January 2022 at 12:00 PM (IST).

  • The BTTC token will be initially listed under Xpress Listing. 

  • Deposits and withdrawals will remain disabled till further communicated


For more information, please refer to the article by the BitTorrent team. BTT Redenomination Plan
