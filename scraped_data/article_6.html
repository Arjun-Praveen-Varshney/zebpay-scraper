We're pleased to inform you that the app has been updated with a new tab.


What's new, you might be curious.


Here's a little preview of the new stuff!

  • A brand new order management tab on the Android & IOS versions of the app.

  • All your exchange and QT orders are in one place.

  • You can check all open, cancelled and traded orders.

  • Statement requests can be placed for all order types. 


Let's have a look at it!

Login to your account on the app.

  • Go to orders and you’ll see the ‘OPEN ORDERS’ tab.

  • You can check all your open and partially open orders for 1 year under this tab. For other dates, you can use the Filter option.






  • Here, you also have the option to filter orders by pair, side, source, order type and date range. 



  • To check the order summary, you can tap on a particular order and you should be able to view order details and trade history.

  • The next feature is Order Cancellation..You can select an individual order and cancel it.



  • Let's navigate to the 'ORDER HISTORY' tab, the following tab.

  1. This tab will show you all cancelled, filled, and partially cancelled orders.

  2. You can filter orders as per your preference.

  3. Order summary and trade history of any order can be checked below order details by clicking on the order.


  • The third and last tab is "TRADE HISTORY". 


On this tab, you can check all your executed orders. You can filter them as per your preference and also check the history.




Hold on, we're not done yet. Please explore one more feature we have for you.


SEND STATEMENT - This is the tab on the top right side of the page. You can request a statement for your order history by clicking the ‘Send Statement’ button and choosing the period and other categories. This statement will be sent to your registered email address with ZebPay. 




With the aid of these new features, you may now conduct extensive trade activities with just the press of a button.
