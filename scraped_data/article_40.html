Search New support ticket Check ticket status Solution home Zebpay Support Articles Crypto Deposit & Withdrawals Issue in sending LTC Print Modified on: Fri, 10 Jul, 2020 at 1:13 PM ZebPay supports sending Litecoin only to the new LTC address format. The new address format begins in an M instead of 3. This format is convertible to the old address format. If you have the old address format, you may use the Litecoin address converter at https://litecoin-project.github.io/p2sh-convert/ to convert it to the new format.
