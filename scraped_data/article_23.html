You can now add/ register upto three bank accounts on our platform. You will be able to deposit fiat currency from and withdraw it into any of these listed/ registered bank accounts. 


This article will show you how to:

  • Add a new bank account
  • Delete an existing bank account 


Add a new bank account


Here are the steps to add additional bank accounts:

  • Go to the 'My Account' section
  • Tap on Bank 
  • Add your new Bank Account details


Go to the 'My Account' section

You can access this by tapping the 'menu' icon on the top left corner of the screen.




Tap on Bank 

You will then see all of the bank accounts registered with us. The green tick-marks against the bank account indicate verified accounts. 


You will be able to add new bank accounts here. 


Click on the button 'Add Bank'.



Add your new Bank Account details


Add the requested details. You will also need to upload one of the following bank documents for proof:


  • Cheque
  • Bank Statement
  • First page of bank passbook


All of these documents have to clearly show the account number, account holder's name, bank name and IFSC code.


Then click on 'Save bank details'. 



That's all that you need to do.


Our system will then auto-verify your bank details. If there was an issue with the information you entered, the system will not be able to complete an auto- verification. 


One of our customer service agents will then need to verify your bank details manually. This manual verification process may take upto 4 working days to complete.  



Delete an existing bank account 


Deleting a registered bank account is just as simple. Once again go to the 'Bank' section by following the steps as before. 


This time, click on the 'Remove' button below each listed account and then click 'Confirm'. 



And you're done!
