Search New support ticket Check ticket status Solution home Zebpay Support Articles Trouble Shooting – Since your device is as unique as you are Cannot view your receiving address Print Modified on: Tue, 21 Jun, 2022 at 1:59 PM In order to view your receiving address, your ZebPay account must be KYC verified.You can resolve this issue by following the below steps for Bitcoin, Litecoin, and Bitcoin Cash.Select the cryptocurrency you wish to receiveTap on the Receive button.A pop-up message will display our network/format. Upon reading the information, click on the box that says 'I am receiving the token in the supported format/ via the supported network' and click on I understand.You can add a new address by tapping on the "Add new address" button.You can name and save the address.In case you are unable to view the receiving address of ERC-20, BEP-20, BNB (BEP-2), XRP, XLM, Atom (Cosmos), Tron (TRX), EOS and other tokens, You can submit a ticket for are support using this link: https://help.zebpay.com/support/tickets/new.
