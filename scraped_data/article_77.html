ZebPay is pleased to announce our support for the Flare (FLR) Airdrop Program for Ripple (XRP) holders.


The snapshot of XRP balance was taken on December 11, 2020.


Flare (FLR) tokens will be distributed to eligible users at a ratio of 1 XRP = 0.1511 FLR.


We are working on distribution of FLR tokens to eligible users and expect it to be happening soon.


We will notify all our eligible members using our formal communication channels on the final date for token distribution in the coming days.


Please note while we support the airdrop, we have decided not to list the FLR token in ZebPay. We will not be featuring the deposit, buy, sell and lend features for FLR tokens. The withdrawal option will be available for the users to withdraw it to other supported wallets.Â
