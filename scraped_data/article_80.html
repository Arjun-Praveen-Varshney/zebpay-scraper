For security reasons, we support iOS versions 10 and above, Android 5 and above for our application.


Our current app versions are 3.21.00 for iOS and 3.18.01 for Android. Please do ensure you have the latest version installed to ensure a seamless mobile trading experience.


What do I do?


If your device is operating on lower mobile OS versions, please upgrade your software to support the latest ZebPay app version. 


For all iOS users, please check this article (http://support.apple.com/HT204204) on how to update the iOS software on your iPhone.


For all Android users, please navigate to Settings >> System updates >> Update system.


Please Note:


Upcoming ZebPay app versions, updates and features will not be supported if your current operating system is lower than the required versions.


Questions? Please write to us at https://help.ZebPay.com/support/tickets/new
