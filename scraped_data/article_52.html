Zebpay Ticker API for currently supported INR, Euro, AUD and crypto-to-crypto pairs:


You can find more details on the APIs provided here.


You can also visit https://www.zebapi.com/pro/v1/market/


  • Buy: Best buy price.
  • Sell: Best sell price.
  • Price Change: Percentage change of price in the last 24hrs.
  • Volume: Volume traded in the pair in the last 24 hrs. The volume is always in the base currency.
  • 24hour High: Highest trade price in the last 24hrs.
  • 24hour Low: Lowest trade price in the last 24hrs.
  • Market: Last trade price.
  • Pair: Trade pair.
  • Virtual Currency: Base currency.
  • Currency: Quote currency.
