For security reasons, after a PIN reset, we place a 24-hour restriction on all outgoing transactions (Fiat and Crypto) as listed below:

  • Send coin

  • Change outgoing transaction

  • Add new address

  • Withdrawal of FIAT


You will be able to perform the below transactions without restriction:

  • Receive cryptocurrencies

  • Trade (Buy/Sell) cryptocurrencies

  • Deposit funds to wallet


After the cooling period of 24 hours after PIN reset, all transactions will be enabled.
