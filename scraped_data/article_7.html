We are pleased to announce the release of the latest mobile app update for our Android customers. This has been created to improve your trading experience. It has an easy-to-use interface and advanced trading tools to meet your demands. 


Let’s see what is in for you.


  • A brand new Chart screen - Our enhanced chart interface will enable you to see the price trends for the selected period and take your trading calls seamlessly.



  • A New User Interface for Guests - The redesigned UI allows you to sign up or log in while using the app in guest mode.


  • KYC interface - Members will be requested to finish their KYC if it is pending in order to comply with our high compliance standards.


  • Market Price change alert - In case the price of a coin changes while you are placing a trade, you will be alerted as shown below.


  • Use the Swipe feature to easily execute trades - you can now swipe right on a specific coin to buy it and swipe left to sell it.


  • A fresh summary screen - Instead of charging you a fee in addition to your selected buy/sale value, your buy or sell amount will now include fees.





  • Order execution - After placing a trade, a new pop-up will now appear.






Our aim is to provide our users with the greatest trading experience possible, and we think the latest upgrade is a big move in that direction.

With these new features, you may now conduct extensive trade activities.
