Stay secure while using a shared device. Use the logout option.


You can logout from ZebPay by tapping on the 3 lines on the top left of the app and under My Account, you will have the option to “Logout”. 

 

 

If you wish to logout from the website, please click on the drop down on the ZebPay icon on the top right of the screen and tap on “Logout”. 

 

When you need to log back in, Click on Let’s go, enter your mobile number, enter OTP sent on your registered mobile number and Pin. Once you login, you will be ready to transact again.
