You can view the equivalent rate of any crypto in fiat currency for all crypto-to-crypto trading pairs.


1. Select the token front the exchange tab and select the crypto-crypto pair to trade (BAT - USDT is given as an example in all below screenshots).

2. You can click on the option to switch to view the price between INR or the crypto in this case USDT to show the price.
