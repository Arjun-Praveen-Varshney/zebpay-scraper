  • TRON withdrawals will be suspended from 21st June 2018
  • TRON deposits will be suspended from 23rd June 2018
  • During the swap event, TRON token trading will not be affected. You can continue to trade TRX on Zebpay exchange.

What do I need to do? It is advised to deposit your TRX tokens on Zebpay exchange to secure your assets before 23rd June 2018.
