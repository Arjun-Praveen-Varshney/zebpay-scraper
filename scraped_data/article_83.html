In order to view your receiving address, your ZebPay account must be KYC verified.


You can resolve this issue by following the below steps for Bitcoin, Litecoin, and Bitcoin Cash.


  1. Select the cryptocurrency you wish to receive

  2. Tap on the Receive button.




  1. A pop-up message will display our network/format. Upon reading the information, click on the box that says 'I am receiving the token in the supported format/ via the supported network' and click on I understand.

  2. You can add a new address by tapping on the "Add new address" button.

  3. You can name and save the address.
