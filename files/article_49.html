Search New support ticket Check ticket status Solution home Zebpay Support Articles App Features View your FIAT and Crypto transaction histories Print Modified on: Mon, 13 Jun, 2022 at 11:48 AM You can view the history of your wallet and cryptocurrency transactions within the app itself.1. FIAT deposit and withdraw transactions:Select "Portfolio" on the home screen.Select "History".You can view all the FIAT (Deposit & Withdrawal) transactions.            2.Cryptocurrency Quick Trade transactions: "Quick Trade":Click on “Quick Trade” at the left bottom corner of the application.Select the coin for which you would like to check the quick trade transaction history.Select the 3 lines on the right top of the app.You can view all the quick trade transactions for the selected coin.Alternatively, you can click on the 3 vertical dots and you can view the quick trade history.Note: Send/ Receive history will not be available for these coins/tokens.            3. Cryptocurrency/Trade transactions: "Exchange":Click on “Exchange” second option at the left corner of the application.Select the coin for which you would like to check the transaction history.Select "History".You can view all the crypto/trade transactions for the selected coin.                  Did you find it helpful?
